Business/Brand Awareness

The goal could be to showcase a company, brand, or product, and inform visitors about your services or offerings. Examples: portfolio websites, company websites, product showcases.

Lead Generation

If you’re looking to convert visitors into customers or clients, the website might focus on capturing leads through contact forms, newsletters, or free offers. Examples: landing pages, SaaS websites.

E-commerce

If you plan to sell products or services online, your website’s goal could be to facilitate online transactions and provide a smooth shopping experience. Examples: online stores, marketplaces.

Community Engagement

Some websites are built to foster a sense of community through forums, social sharing, or discussion. Examples: forums, social networks, event sites.

Education or Content Sharing

If you want to share knowledge, provide resources, or teach something, the goal could be centered around sharing articles, videos, or courses. Examples: blogs, online courses, informational websites.
